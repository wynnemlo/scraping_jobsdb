IT Technical Support Engineer

Ogawa Health Care International (HK) Ltd
Apply Now

Job Highlights

  • Perform day-to-day helpdesk support
  • Troubleshoot IT problems and issues
  • Hardware and software evaluation and procurement

Job Description

Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for set up, maintenance and configuration of IT infrastructure includes network, Window servers, security systems and hardware
  • Perform day-to-day Help-desk support to users, both onsite and remote basis
  • Investigate, diagnose and troubleshoot IT related problems
  • Perform routine IT operations including system monitoring / administration, logs review and data backup
  • Co-ordinate with internal stakeholders and external vendors/contracts to manage multiple projects simultaneously
  • Work with external vendors for hardware and software evaluation and procurement
  • Assist in stock keeping and control of equipment inventory record
  • Assist in execution of IT Projects as required

 Requirements:

  • Higher Diploma holder or above in Computer Studies, Computer Science or related disciplines
  • Min. 5 years of relevant experience in IT infrastructure administration and/or IT support services
  • Hands-on experience in supporting end-users in a Windows environment
  • Familiar with ERP (POS/CRM/SAP) System is an advantage
  • Responsible, self-motivated, and able to work independently
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills
  • Good command of written and spoken English and Mandarin
  • Immediately available is highly preferred

We offer attractive remuneration and career development opportunity. Interested parties, please send your CV together with availability, present and expected salaries by clicking “APPLY NOW”

Company Overview

OGAWA is a market leader established in 1996 which specializes in design, development, marketing of health and wellness equipment.  With its innovative health and wellness products of the highest quality, OGAWA have paved a path in garnering numerous accolades.  

OGAWA currently operates over 700 retail shops in more than 20 countries, including Mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Macau, Singapore, Malaysia, Vietnam and Philippines.  We are now inviting high caliber candidates to become part of our team and to pursue a bright and exciting future.   Let’s join our Big Family!
